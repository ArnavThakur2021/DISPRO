DISPRO Core Services

Our system acts as a central controller connecting multiple information sources to evaluate disaster risk and vulnerability.


Hazard Prediction

Fetches data from weather forecasting systems to predict rainfall (floods), wind speed (cyclones), and temperature spikes (heatwaves).

Vulnerability Analysis

Collects building details like soil type and structure type to determine if a building can withstand earthquakes or landslides.

Intelligent Alerting

Integrates city pollution monitoring (AQI) for immediate detection of pollution and air quality.

Advisory Engine

Generates customized "Do's and Don'ts" safety instructions tailored to specific buildings and GPS locations.

Resource Mapping

Utilizes geospatial data to locate and suggest the nearest emergency teams, hospitals, and fire stations.

Authority Alerts

Synchronizes with NDMA and local news portals for active, verified disaster announcements from authorities.
