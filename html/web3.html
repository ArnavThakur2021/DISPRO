DISPRO: Disaster Protection System

The Disaster Protection System (DISPRO) is an intelligent, multi-source platform designed to mitigate the impact of natural and environmental hazards through real-time data integration and personalized risk assessment. In an era of increasing climate volatility, DISPRO serves as a central controller that harmonizes data from weather services, news outlets, geospatial sensors, and building-specific telemetry to predict and detect impending disasters.

Unlike traditional broad-spectrum alert systems, DISPRO evaluates disaster vulnerability at a granular level, calculating the specific risk posed to individual users and structures. The system's core functionality includes the automated collection and storage of environmental and building data, followed by a rigorous analysis of risk and vulnerability.

System Objectives

  • Predict & Detect: Identify disasters using real-time sensors and news feeds.
  • Evaluate Vulnerability: Assess danger levels specifically for individual buildings or users.
  • Actionable Intelligence: Generate real-time alerts with user-specific "Do’s and Don’ts."
  • Resource Mapping: Suggest nearby emergency support and resources.
