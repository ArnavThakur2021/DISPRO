About DISPRO

Intelligence for Life Safety and Structural Protection

Our Mission

The Disaster Protection System (DISPRO) is an intelligent alert and advisory platform designed to bridge the gap between raw environmental data and actionable user safety. Our goal is to predict disasters, evaluate building-specific risks, and provide real-time, personalized instructions to safeguard lives and property.

The Central Controller

DISPRO acts as a central hub that harmonizes data from multiple critical systems:

NDMA & Local News Portals
Weather Forecasting Sites
On-site Sensors & Alarms
Geospatial Data Systems

Risk Analysis

We analyze hazard zones, such as fault lines and river banks, to determine localized vulnerability mapping.

Building Safety

By assessing soil types and structure parameters, we calculate safety ratings for individual buildings.

Safety Advisories

We deliver customized "Do's and Don'ts" safety instructions directly to the user interface.
